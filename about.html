About this site

Hi! I'm Maria.

I am a fourth year computer science student at the University of Costa Rica and this is my personal website.

I started learning how to code almost six years ago, and ever since my first program I have made sure to leave my signature on everything I have worked on. To me the quality of a program is as important as its usefulness. That's why I always strive to find a balance of both on every project I work on. But not only in my code, outside of my programming expertise I also aspire to put passion and dedication on everything new I do.
