Why computer science?

I started learning how to code in my ninth grade IT class and it was love at first sight. After I scored a 7/7 in that class I knew I had potential to become a professional. I still wasn't convinced though, making simple Java games is one thing but dedicating my career to computers seemed a bit far fetched. Two NGOs have played a huge part on convincing me I had what it took. MenTe En Accion (Women in STEM) and Rocket Girls, both based in Costa Rica were fundamental in my decision to build a career in this profession.

Now I can proudly call myself a woman who codes <3
